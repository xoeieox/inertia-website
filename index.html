DESIGN. PLAY. BUILD ON IT.

One geometry spine runs the whole loop. The same file you design is the file you play, fork, and print — and credit follows it at every hop.

01

Design

Model an original top in CAD and export a STEP file — structured geometry, not triangle-soup mesh.

02

Simulate

Drop it into Arc and battle it in real physics. Watch how the geometry actually behaves before you commit a print.

03

Deposit

Log it in the registry with attribution and your own license + royalty terms attached to the file.

04

Fork & Remix

Anyone can build on it. Every iteration stays linked to the lineage it came from — yours included.

05

Print

Order it from a print-on-demand maker in plastic or metal, straight through the platform.

06

Get Paid

One payment splits along the chain — every designer in the lineage, plus the maker who printed it.

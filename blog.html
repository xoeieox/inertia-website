October 20, 2025 MILESTONE

October 2025 - Momentum Campaign Launch

Public demonstrations of Insight and Axis prototypes. Community reception and what's next.

The Momentum Campaign

After months of development in private, we publicly demonstrated working prototypes of Insight (precision launch analyzer) and Axis (computer vision tournament system) in October 2025.

This wasn't vaporware or concept renders. These were functional prototypes solving real problems for competitive players and tournament organizers.

What We Showed

Insight Prototype

Real-time RPM tracking, launch angle measurement (IMU), and Bluetooth sync to the Inertia App. Compact attachment form factor with live LCD feedback.

Learn more about Insight →

Axis Computer Vision

Real-time battle detection, win classification (Spin Finish, KO, Burst), and automated clip generation. Tournament-ready accuracy for organizers.

See Reddit demo →

Inertia App Demo

We also showcased the Inertia App—the platform that connects Insight, Axis, and tournament infrastructure. Voice input for match recording, Swiss pairing automation, and real-time brackets.

Learn more about the Inertia App →

Community Reception

The response from the competitive Beyblade community exceeded expectations:

  • ✓ 1.2k+ views on Reddit demonstrations within the first week
  • ✓ Positive engagement from competitive players on WBO Forums
  • ✓ Tournament organizers reaching out to join beta programs
  • ✓ Feature requests and integration ideas from the community

The most rewarding feedback? Competitive players who immediately understood the value of objective data. No explaining required—they just got it.

What's Next

The Momentum Campaign demonstrated proof of concept. Now we're focused on production readiness:

November 2025 - Private Beta Testing

Closed beta for Inertia App with select organizers and competitive players. Refining tournament infrastructure based on real-world usage.

December 2025 - Open Beta Launch

Inertia App open beta for organizers and players. Insight production concept art reveal. Axis alpha testing continues with partner organizers.

Q1 2026 - Public App Launch

Free tournament platform for iOS, Android, and web. Foundation for hardware integration launching later in 2026.

Q2-Q3 2026 - Hardware Launches

Insight (Feb beta → Mar-Apr production) and Axis (Q2 beta → Q3 launch). Full ecosystem integration with app platform.

Building the Right Way

We're not rushing hardware to market. Foundation before features. Proof before hype.

The Inertia App launches free in Q1 2026 because tournament infrastructure needs to be solid before hardware integrates with it. Insight and Axis follow when they're production-ready, not when arbitrary deadlines demand it.

This approach takes longer. But competitive players deserve tools that respect the craft—not rushed products that overpromise and underdeliver.

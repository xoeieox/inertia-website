DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to delete your Inertia account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

1

Email delete@inertia.cx from your registered email address

2

Include "Delete My Account" in the subject line

3

We will process your request within 7 days

Send Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

  • • Profile information (username, bio)
  • • Saved combos and parts collection
  • • Tournament participation history
  • • Battle statistics and practice logs

What We Retain

  • • Anonymized match records (for tournament integrity)
  • • Aggregated statistics (no personal identifiers)

Retained data cannot be linked back to your identity after deletion.

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

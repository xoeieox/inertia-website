RELEASE NOTES

What's new in Inertia. Updates, improvements, and the journey forward.

v1.5.2

The Digital Dojo Update

January 2026

"Your digital dojo awaits."

We've rebuilt Inertia from the ground up. This release transforms the app into a complete tournament ecosystem with a beautiful new look, smarter combo management, and tools that work the way you actually play.

What's New

Run Tournaments Your Way

Create and manage tournaments with WBO-compliant settings, Swiss pairing, bracket progression, and live standings. Organizers get a dedicated command center (Shomen) to manage matches, judges, and players—all in real-time.

Light & Dark Themes

Switch instantly between Dawn (light) and Dusk (dark) modes. Every screen has been redesigned to look great in both.

Your Gear, Always Accessible

New Side Trays let you access your cases and combos without leaving the battle screen. Swipe in from the edge to grab a combo, check your deck, or review match history—all mid-battle.

Testing Sessions

A dedicated mode for experimenting with combos. Track wins, losses, and finish types across sets. Tag your combos with community feedback when you're done.

Player Passport

Your identity, beautifully presented. A swipeable card showing your profile, stats, tournament history, and favorite combos.

Holodeck (Tournament Programs)

Build reusable tournament configurations. Mix and match battle modes, formats, and staging rules into "Programs" you can share and reuse.

Smarter Workshop

+Cases: Organize combos into folders that sync to the cloud
+Blade-first grouping: Find combos by blade type
+Mode-changing parts: Eclipse, Turn, and Dual, show correct types
+Batch actions: Select multiple combos at once

Judge Mode Improvements

+Landscape-friendly scoring interface
+Visual setup matching physical stadium orientation
+Camera replay support for reviewing close calls
+OBS broadcast sync for streamers

Challonge Integration

Sync your tournaments to Challonge.com for public brackets without leaving Inertia.

Quality of Life

+Pull-to-refresh everywhere
+Better onboarding for new users
+Full backup and restore in Quarters
+Faster, snappier animations throughout
+214 parts in the database (v4.5 update)

Coming Soon

-Holodeck community sharing
-Garden & Potential rooms
-Public App Store release

Precision Launch Analyzer

Real-time RPM, launch angle, and consistency tracking for competitive Beyblade. Objective data that proves technique over luck.

The Problem

No Objective Launch Metrics

  • •Can't measure RPM or power objectively
  • •No way to track launch angle consistency
  • •Impossible to compare launches across sessions
  • •Technique improvements feel subjective

Competitive Disadvantage

  • •Elite players practice blind without feedback
  • •Can't prove skill development over time
  • •No data to correlate launch quality with wins
  • •Hard to diagnose inconsistent performance

The Solution

Real-Time Launch Analytics

Insight attaches to your launcher and captures precise metrics for every launch. RPM, angle, spin duration, and consistency—all synced to your Inertia App profile for long-term tracking.

What It Measures

  • ✓RPM Tracking: Precise rotational speed per launch
  • ✓Launch Angle (IMU): Tilt and orientation data
  • ✓Spin Duration: How long your Bey stays active
  • ✓Consistency Metrics: Standard deviation across launches

How It Works

  • ✓Compact Attachment: ~120mm × 40mm × 25mm
  • ✓Live Display: 1.28" circular color LCD
  • ✓Bluetooth Sync: Auto-syncs to Inertia App
  • ✓All-Day Battery: ~8 hours, rechargeable USB-C
